Located in Buckead Atlanta, Elbow Room is a tradition

Elbow Room was established in 1999 by Staten Island's own, *Joe Bonano. He has brought the flavors of NYC and serves traditional thin crust pizza amongst other bar fares. This sports bar and grill is the perfect neighborhood spot to catch a few drinks with your friends.
